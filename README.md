# Tool(s) for Shahe calipers

## Remote Viewing
To use, turn on the calipers, then hold down the little round black button on the side of the calipers for a couple of seconds, and then release. You should see a wireless icon flashing on the display. 

[Click here to go to the remote viewing app](https://github.com/shabaz123/shahe_caliper_tool/caliper_tool.html) , and then click on Connect. You should see the calipers listed, you can select them, and then click on Pair. Now, whever you press the round black button briefly, the measurement will be sent to the remote viewing app.

If you wish to stream values constantly, then hold down the yellow power button for a couple of seconds, and then release. The display will cycle through a menu. When the display indicates "Au" (short for Automatic), press the yellow button briefly. Now the display will cycle through some numbers: 0, 1, 2, 4, 8. These represent the number of measurements that will be streamed per second. Wait until the number reaches 8, and then press the yellow button briefly. Now the measurements will update 8 times per second.
